DHL eCommerce | 2019 - 2024

General

I was responsible for designing and prototyping in Figma for both mobile and desktop, focusing on consumer and operational applications. My work included conducting user research, designing illustrations and icons, and leading creative sessions and brainstorms. Additionally, I designed notification emails for both consumers and operations, while collaborating closely with various departments to ensure a cohesive and user-friendly experience.

Consumer products

My DHL App

In collaboration with the product owner and developers, I worked on several projects within the award winning DHL app MyDHLApp. A consumer application focussed on tracking your parcels and creating labels to send parcels. I was involved in introducing new concepts, further developing existing concepts, conducting user research, and facilitating brainstorm and creative sessions with stakeholders and users.

DHL Zelfscanzuil

In collaboration with the product owner and developers, I designed the first version of the 'self-service kiosk.' This product allows users to check in their parcels at the 'kiosk' instead of having to wait in line. After the initial launch, users rated it 9.5/10 for usability.
